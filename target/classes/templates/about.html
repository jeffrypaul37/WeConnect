About Us


WeConnect serves as a hub where urban residents can unite and express their enthusiasm for the wonders of the natural world.



Our Events



Our distinctive gatherings take place throughout Canada, whether it's at a brewery in Halifax, by a lake in Alberta, or within a store in Downtown Toronto, ON.

Wherever our gatherings occur, you'll discover amiable outdoor enthusiasts coming together over maps, guidebooks, beverages, and tales of adventure.

Our Manifesto

WE BELIEVE THAT:


The outdoors are for everyone

...regardless of your background, religion, sexual orientation, race, or gender identity. This inclusivity ensures that you are welcome here.


There's plenty to share

...from our peers and expert guides to exploring public parks and trying out new sports.


Outdoor activities are beneficial

...they contribute to increased happiness, strength, confidence, physical health, and beyond.


We are good for the outdoors

...as our community actively engages in preserving the natural beauty, health, and accessibility of the great outdoors for everyone.


Living in a city is possible.

while still cherishing outdoor activities.
